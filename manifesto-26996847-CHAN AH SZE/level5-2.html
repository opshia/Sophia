Be more positive.

                            Try to stop anthropomorphizing
                            or the animals I know,
                            or at least do it less.

Play games that require abandon.

Get better at maintaining
relationships with friends.
Look at how I am not fully
conscious of my real life,
admit that I am groping in
the dark, overwhelmed by the
consequences of my acts and
that at every moment I am
faced with outcomes I didnot intend.
